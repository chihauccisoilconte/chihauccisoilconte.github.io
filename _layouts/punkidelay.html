Punkidelay

Punkidelay is a kind of stylophone based on Atari punk console.
It's a diy synth designed by the Spain based collective called Familiar . .
I designed this casing for the sinth in a way that any user would be able to put it tegether and use it. The device is made out of lasercutted polycarbonate. you can find some work in progress pics here .
