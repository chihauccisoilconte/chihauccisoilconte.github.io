Wonky Drum Seq

The Wonky Drum Sequencer was design in collaboration with STEIM during project "A Square dreams A Sphere". It is a MIDI step sequencer that process a virtual drum machine.

The instrument it's been used as a guideline to give a 4 days workshop where the participants followed step by step the process of designing the controller.

All the files needed to reproduce the Wonky Drum seq are available here And it is possible to make one using the common Fablab machines. The Wonky Drum Seq is released with a Creative Commons BY-NC-SA licence The project is funded by the Netherlands Creative Industries Fond
