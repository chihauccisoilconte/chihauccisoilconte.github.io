Dispositivo Cinetico MIDI

Dispositivo Cinetico Midi (kinetic MIDI device) is a MIDI controller designed to include a strong physical interaction into a music performance

The characteristics of the D are devised to offer artists the same sound possibilities of the Theremin, while also guaranteeing a wider range of options in terms of stage mobility and performance.

The interface is composed by two main elements: a set of 12 keys that the user have to press to send MIDI notes, and a gyroscope situated in the center pf the device and that can detect the device position.
By moving the device, the performer can send MIDI CC signals, creating a correspondence between gestures and sound effects.

The shape of the device is circular, and made out of two plastic shells that efficiently keep all the electronic components, plus a wooden handle devised to offer a comfortable and pleasant experience while moving the controller.

The interface works as a digital and compact instrument, and it can be personalized according with the artist’s needs and ways of making music.

Being based on open hardware and open software, the artist can disassemble the instrument and re-code the program to customize waves’ shape and frequency, sensors calibration and other specifics. In fact, we encourage you to personalize the Nano Aetherphone by shipping it with assembly instructions.
